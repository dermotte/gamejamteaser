Game Jam @ AAU Klagenfurt

6th Klagenfurt Game Jam
Apr. 26-28, 2019

What is a Game Jam?

The Klagenfurt Game Jams go for 48 hours and are open to everyone who likes games and wants to create, test & talk about games. Starting Friday the topic will be revealed to all participants at the same time and random groups will brainstorm games.

Then, after the ideas are pitched, teams will emerge around ideas and games are to be created. Finally, on Sunday, the projects are presented to the audience and a price for the most awesome projects are awarded.

Who goes to a Game Jam?

The game jam is not restricted to game designers or programmers, but is open to everyone who wants to build games. Each team is in desperate need for visual and audio artist, animation experts, web page writers, storytellers, testers, ...
